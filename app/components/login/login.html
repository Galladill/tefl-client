Dynamic Tools for ESL and TEFL Instructors

Enstruct is a simple web app to help instructors who teach English as a second or foreign language craft dynamic lesson plans for their classes. To get started, simply sign up with your email. Enstruct is completely free, as our goal is to benefit the community at large.
